Hide your monkey in a box if your friends are coming for lunch.

Context

Hide the monkey only if he approves it.

Pre-requisites

  • 1 monkey
  • 1 box

Steps

  1. Grab the monkey
  2. Get him inside the box

    Be sure to find a box bigger than the monkey

  3. Run away
